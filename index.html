About Me

Hi everyone! My name is Alyssa, and I am a lifestyle Photographer. I currently work as a part time Pharmacy Technician, and a part time photographer with the help of my caring husband Lentz.

When people ask me what kind of photos I take, I say “Well, I shoot just about anything!” which is true! You name it: portrait, wedding, engagement, graduation, kids, pets.. the list goes on! My goal is not only to create wonderful photos, but also to create a fun and memorable experience.

Let me ask you this: Do you feel awkward posing in a stuffy studio with more equipment than an operating room?

Most of my clients feel the same way, so I go away with all that and take you to a gorgeous outdoor location where you can relax and just be yourself while I create the most amazing portraits you can imagine—all while having fun!

So if you’re interested in setting up a photo shoot, feel free to contact me. I can’t wait to make great photos with you!
